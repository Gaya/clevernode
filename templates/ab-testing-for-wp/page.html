Measure Conversions of Your Tests

With A/B Testing for WordPress you can serve multiple variants of content to your visitors. This allows you to track which version of your website will lead to more conversions.

A/B Testing for WordPress will serve either version of the test variants and measures the visitors that actually visit the goal page. Let the plugin figure out which of the variants was most popular with your audience.
